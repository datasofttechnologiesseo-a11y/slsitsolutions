Reliable Backup & Recovery Solutions

Data loss costs Indian businesses crores every year. Our automated backup and disaster recovery solutions ensure your critical business data is always protected and recoverable within minutes, not days.

Our Backup & Recovery Solutions

Comprehensive data protection services tailored to keep your business safe and operational.

Automated Backup Systems

Scheduled, hands-free data protection that runs in the background, ensuring your critical files and systems are continuously backed up without manual intervention.

Data Replication

Real-time data mirroring for redundancy across multiple locations, providing seamless failover and ensuring no single point of failure can compromise your data.

Disaster Recovery Planning

Comprehensive recovery strategies tailored to your business, with documented procedures, defined RTOs and RPOs, and regular testing to ensure readiness.

Business Continuity Solutions

Keep operations running during disruptions with robust continuity planning, failover systems, and rapid response protocols that minimize impact on your workflow.

NAS / SAN Storage

Enterprise-grade storage solutions with Network Attached Storage and Storage Area Network configurations designed for high availability, performance, and scalability.

Tape Backup Solutions

Reliable long-term archival storage using modern tape technologies, offering cost-effective, air-gapped protection for compliance and historical data retention needs.
